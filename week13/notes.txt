Defination of LST -> 
   
      An LST, or liquid staking token is a type of token that represents a users staked assets in a proof of stake blockchain.
      when your stake your cryptocurrency you often cannot access or trade it while it's staked. liquid staking allows you to maintain liquidity while still particiates in the staking process.
        1. Staking: When you stake your cryptocurrency with a liquid staking provided, you receive an equivalent amount of LSTs in Return. these tokens represents your staked assests.

        2. Liquidity: since LSTs can be traded or used in other decentralized finance(DEFI) applications, you can take 
           advantages of market opportunities while your original assets remain staked.

        3.Earning reward: while holding LSTs, you still earn staking rewards on the underlying assets, 
          typically reflected in the value of the LST.
